Role Descriptor: Scrum Master
The Scrum Master coaches and guides the Scrum Team and ensures they are able to work efficiently.

Main Description
The Scrum Master is an integral part of the Scrum Team and is responsible for the overall project management tasks with a focus on helping the team to perform optimally. Unlike the more traditional "Project Leader", the Scrum Master acts mainly as a coach and guide to the Scrum Team rather than as a leader. The Team itself is responsible to find the best way to come to each Sprint's objective, the Scrum Master helps them to adhere to the process, set the boundaries and solve issues that block the team from doing their work.
Another important role of the Scrum Master is to shield the team from interruptions during sprints as much as possible.
