Role Descriptor: Product Owner
The Product Owner is the project sponsor's representative in the SW Development project.
Based on Method Role: Product Owner

Main Description

The Product Owner represents the project's sponsor during the SW Development project and is the main resposible for the Product Backlog. He negotiates with the involved sponsor/customer stakeholders to fill the product backlog and prioritize the stories in it.
During the requirements phase(s), he is closely involved in the elicitation and development of requirements, either directly or by bringing the Software Requirement Engineer into contact with stakeholders. He ensures that the backlog is always filled, and that stories at the top (highest priority) have enough detail or requirements to be developed in a Sprint.

The Product Owner also participates in planning sessions ( Release Planning Session, Sprint Planning Session) and project review sessions (Sprint Retrospective, Sprint Review Meeting) so he provide his input and feedback on the planning of upcoming sprints and the outcome of each sprint.

Key Considerations
Ideally, the Product Owner comes from within the customer's organisation. He needs to have the authority to take decisions on development priorities and approval on each sprint's outcome.
