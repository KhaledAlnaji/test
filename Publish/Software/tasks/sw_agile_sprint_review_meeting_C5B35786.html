Task: Sprint Review Meeting
Inspection meeting for project progress at the end of every sprint.
Disciplines: All Software Tasks, MAN.3 - Software Project Management
Purpose
The Sprint Review provides an inspection of project progress at the end of every Sprint. Based on the inspection, adaptations can be made to the project.

      Main Description

      At the end of the Sprint, the team presents the product increment that it has been able to build. Management, customers, users, and the Product Owner assess the product increment. They listen to what the team has to tell about its journey during the Sprint. After this, they determine the best course to take in order reach their intended goals.

      During the meeting, everyone visualises the demonstrated product functionality working in the customer or user environment. As this is visualised, there will be considered what functionality might be added in the next Sprint. The product increment is the focal point for brainstorming.  As the team demonstrates the product increment, it helps the attendees understand the weaknesses and strengths of the product increments, and the difficulties and successes it experienced pulling it together.

      Steps
      Plan meeting
      Plan meeting and invite all stakeholders
      Facilitate the meeting
      Ensure that all attendees can understand the weaknesses and strengths of the product increments, the difficulties and successes it experienced pulling it together.
      Document outcome
      Ensure that the outcome of the meeting is documented and can be used for new sprints.
